Privacy Policy for Resume Around

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Introduction

Resume Around is a resume and CV maker platform. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what choices you have when using our website.

Information We Collect

When you use Resume Around, we may collect the following information:

  • Account details such as full name, email address, password, phone number, city, and profile photo.
  • Resume details such as name, contact details, address, education, skills, work experience, personal details, and uploaded resume photo.
  • Files you upload for your profile or resume.
  • Basic technical information that may be created by the web server, such as browser, device, request time, and page visits.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create, preview, save, edit, and download your resume or CV.
  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To allow you to access saved resumes from your account.
  • To help you reset your password.
  • To improve website features, performance, and user experience.
  • To prevent misuse, unauthorized access, or technical problems.

Resume Data and Uploaded Files

Your resume information and uploaded photos are stored so that you can preview, download, edit, and manage your resume. You should only upload your own information and files that you have permission to use.

Password and Account Security

You are responsible for keeping your login details safe. Do not share your password with others. If you believe your account has been accessed without permission, reset your password immediately.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when required to operate the website, comply with law, protect our rights, prevent fraud, or respond to a valid legal request.

Cookies and Local Storage

Resume Around may use cookies or session storage to keep you logged in and maintain your website session. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some login features may not work properly.

Data Retention

We keep your account and resume data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may update your profile and resume information from your account.

Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile details.
  • You can edit saved resumes.
  • You can reset your password using the forgot password option.
  • You can choose not to upload optional profile or resume photos.

Children's Privacy

Resume Around is intended for users who are old enough to prepare job, education, or professional documents. If a minor uses the website, they should do so with guidance from a parent or guardian.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@resumearound.com or visit the Contact Us page.
